Visual Art Series

As one of the Ministry of Culture's priority policies, the Taiwan Art Bank is managed by the Taiwan Fine Arts Foundation. This project aims to foster artistic creation, support Taiwanese contemporary artists, and cultivate a diverse art environment through art rental. Each year, the Taiwan Art Bank invites members of the professional selection committee to select new collections, with the aim of ensuring that the selected works uphold the high standards and diversity of Taiwanese contemporary art. The Taiwan Art Bank has collected over 2,921 Taiwanese contemporary artworks and offers an artwork rental service exclusively to public and private institutions. These artworks can thus be exhibited in various spaces and embody the values, vitality, and creativity of the institutions that host them.
