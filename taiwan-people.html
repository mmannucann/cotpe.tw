Visual-Art Series

Art Bank Taiwan is an important initiative led by the Ministry of Culture and implemented by the Taiwan Fine Arts Foundation. The project aims to foster artistic creation and support Taiwanese contemporary artists. Each year, Art Bank Taiwan invites professional selection committee members to collaboratively choose new collections, ensuring that the selected works uphold the high standards and diversity of Taiwanese contemporary art. The bank has amassed over 3,000 Taiwanese contemporary artworks. By leasing the artworks, they are displayed in suitable spaces, bringing the value, vitality, and creativity of contemporary art to environments where art is appreciated.
